Robbie Oppenheimer

Father of the Atomic Bomb

Wikipedia

Here is a timeline of Oppenheimer's life

1904 — Born in New York City

1925 — Graduates from Harvard with a degree in Chemistry

1936 — Becomes Physics professor at UC Berkeley

1942 — Recruited to start the Manhattan Project

1943 — Appointed director of Los Alamos Laboratory

July 16, 1945 — Conducts first detonation of an atomic weapon

1947 — Becomes director of Princeton's Institute for Advanced Study, and chair of U.S. Atomic Energy Commission

1967 — Died in Princeton, NJ
